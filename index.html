About Us

∎

Established 1998

We are a small family run business who aim to provide a friendly personal service. We endeavour to supply excellent quality joinery at competitive prices.

Combining a variety of experience, modern materials and a well-equipped workshop we can offer bespoke staircases, doors, windows, cabinets, gates, and all other joinery for your needs.

Who we are

∎

Our partnership consists of two brothers, David and Daniel. Between us we have over 50 years of woodworking experience.

David

"I started my career in New Zealand, apprenticed to a joinery company on the Kapiti coast, making joinery for individual new homes and commercial properties. Furthering my experience in the UK , working for various building companies, a boatyard and local joinery before setting up Kiwi Joinery."

Daniel

"I served my apprenticeship with a New Zealand based building firm, building individual timber framed houses before returning to the UK, gaining experience with local building companies, shopfitting and community projects."
